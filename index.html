Lick Autoplayer (So Killin!)

"The Lick" is a famous musical phrase often used in jazz improvisation. It typically starts on the root note of the current chord or key. While "The Lick" can vary slightly depending on context and interpretation, this program was written to allow aspiring musicians to play this legendary passage simply by pressing a singular key on their keyboard!!!

This box changes the BPM the lick is played back at!
